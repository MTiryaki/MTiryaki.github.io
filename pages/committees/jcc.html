Joint Crisis Cabinet: Greco-Turkish War

After the newly-founded Turkish state won the Battles of İnönü between 11 January-24 July 1921, the Greek Kingdom attacked the Turkish army at the Eskişehir-Kütahya front line. The Turkish army withdrew to east of the Sakarya River, allowing the Greek armies to conquer Kütahya, Afyon and Eskişehir. With their power growing, the Greek armies are planning their last strike. In the meanwhile, there's chaos at the Grand National Assembly of Turkey. Fate of the Anatolian territories are waiting to be written...
