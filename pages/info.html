1. Who can participate in MUNUAL?

- MUNUAL is a student conference that welcomes high school and university students between 14 to 25 years old.

2. Is it obligatory to have experience in MUN or MUN related conferences?

- No, any high school or university student with strong interests in international law and/or politics can apply. Although it is not obligatory to have any experience, it may be desired.

3. How many delegates may represent a country?

- Only one delegate may represent a country in each committee. Multiple delegates can represent the same country in different committees.

4. What is the official language of the conference?

- English is the official language of the conference.

5. Is it obligatory to attend all sessions?

- It is obligatory to attend all sessions to recieve your certification. However, every delegate has the right to miss one session without providing any reason. In any other case, the delegate may ask permission of the Secreteriat through the Committee Directors.

6. What is the dress code policy of MUNUAL?

- MUNUAL requires all delegates to be dressed in a Western business attire, including suits and ties for males.

7. Which committees could I apply for?

- You can apply for all of the committees.
